Our Research Lab

Immune Engineering Lab

Indian Institute of Technology, Mandi

The Immune Engineering Laboratory at IIT Mandi focuses on computational immunology and vaccine design. Our team specializes in developing integrated bioinformatics tools that streamline the process of vaccine development through advanced computational methods.

Lab Director

Dr. Amit Prasad

Associate Professor, IIT Mandi

Dr. Amit Prasad leads the Immune Engineering Lab at IIT Mandi, focusing on computational immunology and vaccine design. With over 15 years of experience in the field, he supervises research projects addressing challenges in vaccine development.
