Privacy Policy

Understand how UniVax handles your data and integrates with external resources for scientific analysis.

Introduction

This policy details data practices for UniVax, an automated framework for vaccine candidate screening. Your privacy and trust are paramount.

Information Collected

At UniVax, we prioritize user privacy. The only data we collect is the FASTA file you upload for analysis. No personal information, login credentials, or identifiable data is required to access or use the platform.

Purpose of Data Use

Data is used to: process your analyses, respond to inquiries, improve UniVax, and ensure service security.

Third-Party Tools & Data Sources

UniVax operates as an aggregator and orchestrator of various publicly available bioinformatics tools and databases. Our platform uses web scraping and API integrations to interface with these third-party resources (e.g., AlgPred 2.0, Phobius, ABCPred, NetCTL Servers, IEDB) to conduct comprehensive analyses.

When your sequence data is transmitted to these external tools, it is subject to their individual privacy policies and terms of use. We strongly advise reviewing their documentation for data handling details.

Mandatory Scientific Citation

Results generated through UniVax rely on underlying external tools and scientific methodologies.

If you utilize UniVax's results in any scholarly work (publications, presentations, etc.), it is mandated by good scientific practice to properly acknowledge and cite the original authors, their publications, and the official websites of each specific tool or database that contributed to your analysis. This ensures appropriate attribution and acknowledges foundational scientific contributions.

Data Retention

Your FASTA file is stored temporarily and periodically deleted from our servers. Other than this, UniVax does not store any user data. Anonymized usage logs may be retained solely for performance analysis.

Policy Modifications

This policy may evolve. Any updates will be posted on this page.
