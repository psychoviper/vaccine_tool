Unified Vaccine Development Platform

UniVax streamlines vaccine development by integrating allergenicity testing, antigen analysis, epitope prediction, and more into a single unified workflow.

Integrated Bioinformatics Tools

Allergenicity Testing

Identify protein sequences that may trigger allergic responses using known allergen data and predictive screening techniques.

Antigenicity Test

Identify protein regions likely to be recognized as antigens by the immune system during the immune recognition process.

Signal Peptide Detection

Detect short peptide sequences that guide proteins for secretion outside the cell using biological sequence analysis.

Epitopes Prediction

Predict specific regions within proteins that may act as B-cell or T-cell epitopes during immune response activation.

Toxicity Assessment

Analyze protein sequences to detect toxic regions that could potentially harm the host or trigger adverse reactions.

Comprehensive Results

Integrate outputs from all modules to provide a complete report useful for designing safe and effective vaccines.
